About UBEC Protocol

"I am because we are" - Ubuntu Philosophy in Economic Practice

Mission & Vision

The Ubuntu Bioregional Economic Commons (UBEC) Protocol represents a fundamental reimagining of economic relationships, grounded in the ancient African philosophy of Ubuntu and powered by modern blockchain technology.

Ubuntu Philosophy

Ubuntu teaches us that individual wellbeing is inseparable from collective wellbeing. "I am because we are" - this profound truth shapes every aspect of the UBEC Protocol.

Project Status

The UBEC Protocol is currently at 85-90% completion, with all four tokens deployed on the Stellar mainnet and operational since October 21, 2025.
