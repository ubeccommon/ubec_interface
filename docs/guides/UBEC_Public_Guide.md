## How the System Evaluates You: The Holonic Score

Unlike traditional systems that only measure how much money you have, UBEC evaluates your participation across **five interconnected principles** drawn from Ubuntu philosophy:

### 1. **Diversity (20% of your score)**
Are you bringing unique value to the network? Do you contribute something distinctive?

### 2. **Reciprocity (25% of your score)**
Are you engaged in balanced giving AND receiving? Do you participate in mutual exchanges?

### 3. **Mutualism (25% of your score)**
Are you forming relationships where everyone benefits? Do your interactions create mutual gain?

### 4. **Regeneration (20% of your score)**
Are you creating sustainable positive impact? Do your actions make the system healthier over time?

### 5. **Interdependence (10% of your score)**
Are you strengthening network connections? Do you recognize and support the web of relationships?

## How Distribution Works: The 75/20/5 Rule

One of UBEC's core principles is that tokens should be distributed in a way that benefits the whole community, not concentrated in a few hands. All UBEC tokens follow this distribution:

- **5% to Operations:** Covers system maintenance and development

## Why Four Tokens Instead of One?

You might wonder: "Why not just use one cryptocurrency?"

The answer lies in **preventing over-optimization**. In traditional economies, everyone optimizes for one thing (money), which creates imbalances:
- People accumulate rather than circulate
- Short-term gain trumps long-term health
- Individual profit matters more than community wellbeing
- Extraction is rewarded over regeneration

By having four complementary tokens, UBEC ensures you can't "win" by optimizing just one dimension. You need:
- **Access** (Air) to participate
- **Reciprocity** (Water) to exchange fairly
- **Stability** (Earth) for lasting relationships
- **Transformation** (Fire) to create positive change

This mirrors how nature worksâ€”you can't have a healthy forest by maximizing only sunlight, or only water, or only soil. You need all elements in balance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to understand blockchain to use UBEC?
**A:** No. Just like you don't need to understand TCP/IP to use email, you don't need to understand blockchain to use UBEC. The system will have user-friendly interfaces.

### Q: Can I convert UBEC tokens to regular money?
**A:** The specifics of this will be determined by the community as part of governance. The goal is for UBEC to be valuable within its ecosystem, not necessarily as a way to extract value out of the community.

### Q: What if I make a mistake with a transaction?
**A:** Blockchain transactions are irreversible, so it's important to double-check addresses. However, within the UBEC community, there will be processes for resolving disputes and addressing mistakes through community governance.

### Q: How much do tokens cost?
**A:** This is still being determined. The focus is on fair initial distribution rather than creating investment speculation.

### Q: Can I use UBEC anywhere, or just in certain places?
**A:** UBEC works anywhere you can access the Stellar blockchain (globally), but it's designed to be most valuable within communities that have adopted it and are actively participating.

### Q: What stops someone from gaming the system?
**A:** Multiple things:
- The five-principle evaluation is hard to fake (you can't score high on reciprocity without actually reciprocating)
- Verification requirements for transformative actions
- Community governance can flag and address abusive behavior
- The system values long-term participation patterns, not one-time tricks

### Q: Is this open source?
**A:** Yes, the project is preparing for open source release. All code will be publicly available for review and audit.

### Q: Can I build applications on top of UBEC?
**A:** Yes! A REST API is planned, enabling developers to create apps, services, and tools that leverage UBEC protocols.
