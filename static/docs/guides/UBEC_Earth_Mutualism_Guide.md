## Earning UBECgpi Tokens

UBECgpi tokens are earned through demonstrated mutualism. The system tracks:

### 1. Relationship Pairs

Every mutualistic relationship is recorded between two accounts:

```
Account A ↔ Account B
```

The system monitors:
- How often do they interact?
- Is there mutual benefit?
- How strong is the relationship?

### 2. Mutual Benefit Scoring

The **Mutual Benefit Score** (0.0 - 1.0) measures whether both parties gain:

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 0.0 - 0.3 | One-sided relationship |
| 0.3 - 0.5 | Unbalanced but some mutual benefit |
| 0.5 - 0.7 | Moderately mutualistic |
| 0.7 - 0.9 | Strong mutual benefit |
| 0.9 - 1.0 | Exemplary mutualism |

### 3. Relationship Strength

**Relationship Strength** (0.0 - 1.0) combines:
- Frequency of interactions
- Balance of exchanges
- Consistency over time
- Depth of collaboration

## Distribution Compliance

### The 65/30/5 Model

UBECgpi enforces strict distribution targets to ensure community control:

| Category | Target | Purpose |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **Community Participants** | 65% | Crowd-funding, regenerative projects |
| **Stewardship Reserve** | 30% | Long-term sustainability |
| **Administration** | 5% | Liquidity and operations |

### Compliance Monitoring

The system continuously monitors distribution:

- **On target** — Distribution within ±5% of targets
- **Warning** — Deviation of 5-10% triggers alerts
- **Corrective action** — >10% deviation requires rebalancing

This ensures the Earth token truly serves as a stable community foundation.

## Stability Metrics

The system tracks overall ecosystem stability through:

### Balance Concentration

How evenly are tokens distributed?
- **Low concentration** = Healthy, distributed ownership
- **High concentration** = Risk of imbalance

### Distribution Health

Is the 65/30/5 model being maintained?
- **Healthy** = Within targets
- **At risk** = Drifting from targets

### Holder Count

How many participants hold Earth tokens?
- More holders = More stable foundation
- Fewer holders = Concentration risk

### Stability Index

A composite score (0.0 - 1.0) measuring overall stability:
- Distribution health
- Balance concentration
- Holder diversity
- Long-term relationship durability

## Troubleshooting

**"My relationships aren't being recognized"**
- Ensure exchanges are on-chain (recorded on Stellar)
- Check that both parties have trustlines established
- Verify consistent interaction patterns
- Review mutual benefit in your exchanges

**"My mutual benefit score is low"**
- Assess whether both parties are truly gaining
- Look for imbalances in your exchanges
- Discuss openly with your partner
- Adjust the relationship for greater mutuality

**"I don't have long-term partners yet"**
- Mutualism takes time to build
- Start with consistent reciprocal exchanges
- Invest in reliability and trust
- Focus on quality over quantity
