## Your Journey Begins Here

Starting with UBEC is simple. Follow these steps to join our growing community of 640+ participants from around the world.

### Step 1: Create Your Stellar Wallet

UBEC tokens live on the Stellar blockchain. You'll need a Stellar wallet to receive, hold, and use your tokens.

**Recommended Wallets:**

| Wallet | Best For | Platform | Link |
|--------|----------|----------|------|
| **Solar Wallet** | Beginners | Mobile & Desktop | [solarwallet.io](https://solarwallet.io/) |
| **Lobstr** | Easy mobile use | Mobile-first | [lobstr.co](https://lobstr.co/) |
| **Freighter** | Browser integration | Chrome/Firefox | [freighter.app](https://freighter.app/) |
| **Albedo** | Advanced users | Browser-based | [albedo.link](https://albedo.link/) |

**Creating Your Wallet:**

1. Download your chosen wallet app or visit the website
2. Click "Create New Wallet"
3. **CRITICAL:** Write down your secret key (56 characters starting with "S")
   - Use paper, not digital storage
   - Store in a safe, secure location
   - This is your only way to recover your wallet—lose it, lose everything
4. Set a PIN or password for daily access
5. Your public key (starting with "G") is now ready to share

> 📋 **See:** [UBEC Onboarding User Guides — Technical Setup Section](/docs/guides/onboarding-user-guides#technical-setup)

---

### Step 2: Activate Your Wallet

New Stellar wallets need approximately 2 XLM (about $0.20-0.50 USD) to activate.

**Options for Activation:**

1. **UBEC can activate it for you** — Ask during onboarding (easiest option)
2. **Purchase XLM** on an exchange (Coinbase, Binance, etc.) and send to your address
3. **Ask another UBEC member** to send you activation XLM

---

### Step 3: Establish Your UBEC Trustline

Before you can receive UBEC tokens, you need to "trust" them. This is a Stellar security feature that prevents spam tokens from being sent without your permission.

**Setting Up the UBEC Trustline:**

1. In your wallet, go to "Assets" or "Manage Assets"
2. Click "Add Asset" or "Add Trustline"
3. Enter the token details:

```
Asset Code:  UBEC
Issuer:      GDPNB7S3IOM2J6C3NA2QG4TQAUCRZXPJJ4HSCCSIKELEH7ORUCX5UB2VN
```

4. Confirm the trustline (costs a tiny amount of XLM)
5. You're now ready to receive UBEC tokens!

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** Once you've established your UBEC trustline, you can set up trustlines for all four tokens to be ready for full ecosystem participation.

---

### Step 4: Receive Your First UBEC Tokens

Once your wallet is set up and trustlines established:

1. Share your public key (the "G" address) with UBEC
2. Verify via the onboarding process
3. Receive your first UBEC tokens
4. Welcome to the commons! 🎉

## Security Best Practices

### ✅ DO

- Write down your secret key on paper
- Store secret key in a safe, secure location
- Make multiple copies stored separately
- Use a PIN/password for daily wallet access
- Keep wallet software updated
- Verify addresses before sending
- Start with small test transactions

### ❌ DON'T

- Ever share your secret key with anyone
- Store secret key digitally (no screenshots, photos, cloud storage)
- Send large amounts without testing first
- Click suspicious links about "urgent wallet issues"
- Give wallet access to untrusted apps

> ⚠️ **UBEC will NEVER ask for your secret key.** If anyone claims to be from UBEC and asks for it, it's a scam.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

**"I can't receive tokens"**
- Verify your UBEC trustline is established correctly
- Check that you gave UBEC the correct public key (starts with "G")
- Make sure your wallet is activated (has minimum XLM balance)
- Contact support with your public key (never your secret key!)

**"Transaction failed"**
- Confirm you have enough XLM for transaction fees
- Verify the recipient address is correct
- Check that the recipient has a trustline for the token you're sending

**"I lost my secret key"**
- Unfortunately, there is no recovery possible without your secret key
- This is why secure backup is critical before funding your wallet

> 📋 **See:** [Full Troubleshooting Guide](/docs/guides/onboarding-user-guides#troubleshooting-common-issues)
