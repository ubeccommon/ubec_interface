## How Regeneration Works

### The Transformation Score

Every transformative action is evaluated across multiple dimensions:

| Factor | What It Measures | Weight |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| **Impact Scale** | How many people/systems affected | High |
| **Regeneration Score** | Depth of healing and renewal | High |
| **Catalytic Multiplier** | Does it spark further change? | Medium |
| **Verification** | Community confirmation | Required |

### Transformation Score Calculation

```
Transformation Score = 
    (Impact Scale × 0.3) + 
    (Regeneration Score × 0.4) + 
    (Catalytic Factor × 0.3)
```

This score determines your UBECtt reward.

## Regeneration Scoring

The **Regeneration Score** (0.0 - 1.0) measures how deeply your action heals and renews:

| Score | Level | Description |

| 0.0 - 0.2 | Minimal | Some positive effect |

## Troubleshooting

**"My action wasn't verified"**
- Ensure you have sufficient documentation
- Reach out to people who witnessed your action
- Provide clear evidence of impact
- Ask for feedback on why verification failed

**"My regeneration score seems low"**
- Consider the depth of healing in your action
- Look for ways to create more lasting change
- Focus on systemic impact, not just surface
- Document long-term effects over time

**"I don't know what transformative action to take"**
- Look for what needs healing around you
- Ask your community what would help most
- Start with small, local actions
- Build on your existing skills and relationships
